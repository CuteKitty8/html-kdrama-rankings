Dena's Top K-Dramas!

  1. A Korean Odyssey

    • Fantasy
    • Romance
    • Horror
    • Comedy

    • It is based on a Chinese tale. It's about a girl who can see spirits. One day, she meets a fairy but he disappears and she waits for him.

      Years later, when she is an adult, she meets the fairy again and an amazing story unfolds.

  2. My Mister

    • Drama
    • Realistic
    • Artistic
    • Understated

    • This movie has two main characters. One, an impoverished young woman, the other, a moderately sucessful but lonely architect/engineer.

      There is a lot to say about the story but it is best just to watch it unfold.

      It is really about the authentic friendship between two people that are from completely different worlds. As well as about the struggles we all face and how we overcome them through the support of loved ones.

  3. The Red Sleeve

    • Drama
    • Romance
    • Suspense
    • Historical

    • Set in the Joseon period and based on the real-life romance between King Jeongjo and Concubine Uibin Sung (their Royal Names).

      The story is primarily from the perspective of palace maid, Sung Deok Im, who is an incredibly kind, strong, and gracious person.

      She quickly falls for the Crown Prince, but, despite her sincere love, withdraws from him because she does not want to live as a concubine.

  4. Black

    • Crime
    • Suspense
    • Supernatural
    • Romance

    • It is about grim reapers and a woman who can see death.

      If someone is going to die soon, she sees the black shadow of death on them. If she touches them, she can see how they will die.

      One day, she meets her long-lost childhood friend who happens to be a cop in search of something himself.

      The two pair up and work together to stop the ill fated futures of those with the shadow on them, or so she thinks....
